Top 3 Tour Spots in Tangub

1.Panguil Bay Bridge

Description 1: Once completed, this bridge will connect Tangub City to Tubod, Lanao del Norte, becoming the longest bridge in the Philippines.

It is expected to boost tourism and trade by providing easier access to and from Tangub, while offering stunning views of the bay.

2.Sinanduloy Cultural Center

A cultural hub that showcases the rich heritage of Tangub through performances, workshops, and exhibits.

center is home to the award-winning Sinanduloy Cultural Troupe, known for their excellence in performing arts and traditional dances.

3.Hoyohoy Highland Stone Chapel Adventure Park

A picturesque park located in the highlands of Tangub, offering breathtaking views of Panguil Bay and surrounding landscapes.

The park features adventure activities such as ziplining, trekking, and the unique Highland Stone Chapel, perfect for nature and thrill-seekers.
