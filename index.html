Welcome to Tangub City

Tangub City, a fourth-class component city in Misamis Occidental, Philippines, is famously known as the “Christmas Symbol Capital of the Philippines.” This title stems from its annual Christmas Symbols Festival, a grand celebration held every December that attracts visitors from across the country. The festival features dazzling light displays, intricate decorations, and cultural performances, making it a must-visit destination during the holiday season. Located along Panguil Bay in Northern Mindanao, Tangub offers scenic views of lush hills, farmland, and coastal landscapes, providing a peaceful rural ambiance.

The city’s economy thrives on agriculture, with rice, corn, and coconuts as its primary products, alongside a thriving fishing industry due to its coastal location. Tangub is also known for eco-tourism, serving as a gateway to Mount Malindang, a protected area ideal for trekking and nature exploration. Education plays a significant role in the city’s development, with institutions like the Northwestern Mindanao State College of Science and Technology (NMSCST) catering to the region’s academic needs. Combining festive traditions, natural beauty, and a strong agricultural base, Tangub City is a unique blend of culture and progress.

Mission

To uplift the quality of life for all people in Tangub City by delivering excellent public services, promoting quality education, strengthening the local economy, preserving the city’s rich culture, and protecting its natural resources for future generations. We aim to foster a community that is inclusive, sustainable, and filled with opportunities for growth and success.

Vision

Tangub City envisions itself as a vibrant and progressive community, renowned for its unity, rich traditions, and outstanding festivals. We aspire to be a model city where people live peacefully, enjoy a clean and healthy environment, and have access to education, livelihood, and opportunities that empower them to build a brighter future.

Core Values

  • Unity - Working together as one community.
  • Service - Putting people’s needs first.
  • Integrity - Doing what is right and honest.
  • Progress - Striving for growth and improvement.
  • Excellence - Doing our best in everything we do.
